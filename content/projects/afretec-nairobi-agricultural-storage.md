# Decentralised Agricultural Storage & Logistics Platform

Built during the 2024 AFRETEC University of Nairobi Student Makerthon, this project addressed post-harvest losses and market access for small-scale farmers.

## Concept
- Modular agricultural storage infrastructure.
- A supporting digital system for logistics, visibility, and coordination.
- Focus on decentralised access rather than a single central warehouse model.

## 2024 Makerthon Context
The University of Nairobi AFRETEC article documents the 2024 Makerthon as a collaborative innovation event involving students from the University of Nairobi and Wits.
