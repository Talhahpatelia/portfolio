# Gotcha (EdTech Proctoring)

Gotcha is an EdTech proctoring project focused on assessment integrity in online and offline environments.

## Product Direction
- Autonomous cheat detection for assessment workflows.
- Practical teacher and institution workflows rather than only detection technology.
- MVP and deployment-oriented development across mobile and backend tooling.

## 2023 IEEE Recognition
Gotcha won first place at the IEEE Entrepreneurship Prospectors workshop at Wits Innovation Centre in 2023. The IEEE article identifies the project as a venture by Talhah Patelia and Jesse Thornburg focused on autonomous cheat detection.
