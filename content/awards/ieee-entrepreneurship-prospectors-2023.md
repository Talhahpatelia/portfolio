# IEEE Entrepreneurship Prospectors Workshop - 1st Place

Won first place at the IEEE Entrepreneurship Prospectors workshop at the Wits Innovation Centre in 2023, presenting Gotcha with Jesse Thornburg.

## What Was Recognised
- Built and presented Gotcha as an EdTech venture focused on autonomous cheat detection for online and offline examinations.
- Refined the pitch, validation path, and execution plan during the workshop sprint.
- Combined education-domain insight, product thinking, and technical feasibility into a first-place pitch.

## Source
The IEEE Entrepreneurship article from July 2023 identifies Talhah Patelia and Jesse Thornburg as the first-place winners for Gotcha.
