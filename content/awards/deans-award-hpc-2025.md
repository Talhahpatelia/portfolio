## Context: Wits High Performance Computing (HPC)

### What is the Wits HPC Team?

The **Wits High Performance Computing Team** is a competitive, research-driven student team that:
- Designs, builds, and optimises HPC clusters
- Runs real-world scientific workloads under strict **power and performance constraints**
- Competes against the **top universities globally**

Participation requires deep skills in:
- Linux systems and cluster administration
- Parallel programming (MPI, OpenMP, CUDA)
- Performance tuning and benchmarking
- Scientific workloads and optimisation
